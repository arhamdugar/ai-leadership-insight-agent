# FY2024 Annual Report — Acme Corporation

## Executive Summary

Acme Corporation delivered strong financial performance in FY2024, with revenue reaching $2.4 billion,
representing 14% year-over-year growth. Net income grew to $380 million (+18% YoY), and EBITDA
margin expanded to 22.5% from 20.8% in FY2023. These results reflect successful execution of our
global growth strategy and continued operational efficiency improvements.

## Revenue Performance

### Full Year Revenue: $2.4B (+14% YoY)

Regional revenue breakdown for FY2024:
- Americas: $1.1B (+18% YoY) — strongest performing region
- EMEA: $960M (+12% YoY) — steady recovery post-macroeconomic headwinds
- APAC: $340M (+4% YoY) — underperformed vs $390M target

Quarterly revenue progression:
- Q1 FY2024: $530M (+15% YoY)
- Q2 FY2024: $590M (+16% YoY)
- Q3 FY2024: $640M (+14% YoY, +8% QoQ)
- Q4 FY2024: $640M (+11% YoY, flat QoQ)

The Q4 deceleration is attributable to APAC segment underperformance and delayed deal closures
in the enterprise segment.

## APAC Segment Analysis

The APAC segment generated $340M in FY2024, falling $50M short of the $390M target. Key drivers:
- Enterprise deal delays in Japan and South Korea (Q3–Q4)
- Competitive pressure from regional incumbents in Southeast Asia
- Currency headwinds (JPY depreciation, -6% impact on reported revenue)

APAC market share: 8.2% (down from 9.1% in FY2023). The Southeast Asian sub-region presents
significant untapped potential, estimated at $85B TAM by FY2026.

## Americas Segment

Americas delivered $1.1B, growing 18% YoY — driven by:
- Enterprise software segment: $620M (+22% YoY)
- Managed services: $280M (+15% YoY)
- Professional services: $200M (+8% YoY)

Key wins: 47 enterprise accounts added in FY2024, average contract value $2.1M (+34% YoY).
Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 118% in Americas.

## EMEA Segment

EMEA contributed $960M (+12% YoY). The UK and Germany markets outperformed:
- UK: $290M (+15% YoY)
- Germany: $245M (+14% YoY)
- Rest of EMEA: $425M (+9% YoY)

EMEA EBITDA margin: 24.1% (above company average), reflecting operational maturity.

## Strategic Risk Factors

1. **APAC underperformance**: Continued competitive pressure and currency risk.
   Mitigation: Targeted M&A review for Southeast Asian market entry.

2. **Customer concentration**: Top 20 customers represent 38% of revenue.
   Mitigation: SMB segment expansion initiative launched Q4 FY2024.

3. **Macroeconomic headwinds**: Global interest rate environment affecting enterprise budgets.
   Mitigation: Multi-year contract structures; 72% of ARR is multi-year.

4. **Talent and retention**: Engineering attrition rate at 14% (industry average 12%).
   Mitigation: Compensation re-benchmarking and equity refresh approved for FY2025.

## Balance Sheet Highlights

- Cash and equivalents: $890M (up from $710M)
- Total debt: $420M (Net cash positive: $470M)
- Free Cash Flow: $485M (+28% YoY)
- Share buyback: $150M completed in FY2024

## FY2025 Guidance

- Revenue: $2.75B–$2.85B (+15%–19% YoY)
- EBITDA Margin: 23%–24%
- Expected APAC recovery: $420M–$440M target
- Southeast Asia market entry: Q2 FY2025 (pending board approval)
- R&D Investment: 14% of revenue (AI/ML product roadmap)
