# Q3 FY2024 Quarterly Report — Acme Corporation

## Quarter at a Glance

Q3 FY2024 revenue: $640M, +14% year-over-year, +8% quarter-over-quarter.
This represents a deceleration from Q2's +16% YoY growth rate, driven by APAC softness.

## APAC Quarter Deep Dive

APAC Q3 revenue of $108M fell $22M below the $130M quarterly target. Primary factors:
- Japan enterprise: 3 large deals delayed to Q4 ($15M combined)
- Southeast Asia: competitive displacement by RegionalTech Inc. ($7M lost)
- India: outperformed at $31M (+21% YoY), bright spot in region

APAC pipeline health: $340M for Q4, with 65% probability-weighted.

## Americas Q3 Highlights

Americas reached $285M, beating target by $5M:
- New enterprise logos: 14 in Q3 (highest in 6 quarters)
- Average deal size: $2.3M (+15% YoY)
- NRR Americas: 121%

## EMEA Q3 Highlights

EMEA at $247M, slight beat:
- UK: $74M (+16% YoY)
- Germany: $63M (+13% YoY)
- Nordics expansion: $28M (new market entry, Q3 FY2024 launch)

## Operational Metrics

- Total ARR: $2,180M (Q3 exit rate, +18% YoY)
- Gross Dollar Retention (GDR): 94.2%
- NRR Group: 115%
- Headcount: 12,400 (net +180 in Q3)
- R&D headcount: 3,800 (31% of total)

## Cash Flow — Q3 FY2024

- Operating Cash Flow: $128M
- Free Cash Flow: $112M
- Cash balance: $820M

## Key Management Commentary

CEO Statement: "Q3 demonstrates the strength of our Americas and EMEA franchises.
We are taking targeted action on APAC, including a strategic review of Southeast Asia
market entry options. The pipeline for Q4 remains strong and we are on track for
full-year guidance."

CFO Statement: "Margin execution has been excellent. Our EBITDA margin of 23.1% in Q3
exceeded our own targets. We entered Q4 with record pipeline and healthy cash generation."
